Our Legacy

A Legacy of Excellence

Established in 1997, Rajalakshmi Engineering College has been at the forefront of technical education in Tamil Nadu. With NAAC A+ accreditation, NBA-accredited programs, and autonomous status granted by Anna University, REC stands as a beacon of quality engineering education.

Our 135-acre campus, equipped with world-class infrastructure, over 550 expert faculty, and 50+ research centers, provides an unparalleled environment for holistic development.
